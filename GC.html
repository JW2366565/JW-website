Graphic Communication

Project 1

Double Exposure

Double Exposure is a great way to get creative with photos. Bringing two or more photos can be beautiful, nostalgic, or unsettling - depending on what photos you use. We were required to visually communicate a concept by creatively combining an image of ourself with one or more chosen images. The aim of this project was to practice basic photo editing skills, and to create a double exposure effect on photoshop. Apply creative and technical skills to create an abstract digital artwork.



Project 2

Book Design

We needed to use a range of photoshop tools and a variety of image manipulation techniques to design a book cover for a book we already red. We needed to extract and re-mix a combination of found bitmap imagery to create a mixed-collage for our book cover design using a range of photo manipulation, blending and compositional techniques. The aim was to practice editing and photographic manipulation skills in Adobe Photoshop. Creative problem solving and decision making conceptualize and design a creative book cover concept and layout. To create a mixed-media collage and apply image manipulation, blending techniques and experiment with compositional techniques for conceptual communication development.
